Safety And Medical Advisory Council

BOSM-2022

Greetings! We are SMAC, an autonomous student body surveying medical facilities, readiness, and safety and security issues on campus for the students and staff and BITS Pilani, Pilani campus.

We are also responsible for the medical needs of any external students who attend our festivals. At our tent we have medical supplies and trained paramedics on standby to assist in any medical emergenices. We also have an ambulance on call if required.

In case of any emergency please contact us at the given numbers or come to our tent immediately.
